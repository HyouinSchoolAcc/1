使命Our Mission

这已经不仅仅是制作一个动漫角色那么简单了。 It's not just about making an anime waifu anymore.

这是一个关于团结现代一代人对抗孤独的故事；激励大家去学习、去锻炼、去发现建立关系的美好。培养找寻快乐并与他人分享的习惯。 It's a story about unifying the modern generation against loneliness; to learn and exercise, to see the good in building a relationship. Creating a habit of finding joy and sharing it with others.

🧠 我们是基础模型公司 🧠 We Are a Foundational Model Company

我们正在试着从零开始构建和训练大型语言模型，专注于改进基础模型本身。不是套壳，不是微调现有模型——我们在尝试创造全新的底层架构，让AI真正理解情感、记忆和人际关系。 We are building and training large language models from the ground up, focused on improving the fundamental model itself. No wrappers, no fine-tuning existing models—we're creating new foundational architecture that truly understands emotion, memory, and human connection.

我们在招募 We Are Hiring

数据质量组 Data Quality Team

  • 给写手分配工作，规划创作任务
  • 设计人物关系、场景与设定，为写手提供创作框架
  • 审核对话质量，确保自然、真实、符合人物动机
  • 指导写手优化与改进对话内容
  • Assign tasks to writers and plan creative work
  • Design character relationships, scenarios, and settings as creative frameworks
  • Review dialogue quality for authenticity and character consistency
  • Guide writers to optimize and improve dialogue content

数据扩张组 Data Expansion Team

  • 招揽与培训写手，扩大创作团队
  • 负责写手薪酬管理，处理工资发放与结算
  • 与写手们沟通宣传与扩张写手与数据数量，并引导他们创作
  • 建立写手社区与激励机制，提升团队积极性
  • Recruit and train writers to expand the creative team
  • Manage writer compensation and handle payment processing
  • Communicate with writers to promote growth and guide their creative work
  • Build writer community and incentive systems to boost team engagement

模型训练与架构组 Model Training & Architecture Team

  • 负责模型训练、微调与优化
  • 设计并实现模型架构与训练流程
  • 研究并应用最新的 AI 技术提升对话质量
  • Responsible for model training, fine-tuning, and optimization
  • Design and implement model architecture and training pipelines
  • Research and apply latest AI technologies to improve dialogue quality

商务合作组 Business Collaboration Team

  • 拓展商业合作伙伴与市场机会
  • 负责项目推广与商务洽谈
  • 建立战略合作关系，推动产品落地
  • Expand business partnerships and market opportunities
  • Responsible for project promotion and business negotiations
  • Establish strategic collaborations to drive product deployment
